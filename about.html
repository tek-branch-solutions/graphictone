Résumé

I am a Savannah College of Art and Design graduate, with a BFA in Graphic Design. What can I say that my work doesn't? I enjoy, branding and packaging mostly, and like to build things. I like to add a one-of-a-kind aesthetic to my projects. My design process is very hands on. I believe in trial and error, and things don't just happen over night, but good ideas do! Creative thinking and hard work go into all of my designs. I enjoy printmaking , book binding, production and Columbian coffee. I work well under pressure and have all of the qualifications of a dedicated designer: great communication skills, works well with others, open to constructive criticism, works long hours, not enough sleep and a mind for design.
